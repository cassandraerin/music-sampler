Paramore was originally a christian alternative rock band formed in 2004. The founding members were Hayley Williams, Josh Farro, Zac Farro and Jeremy Davis. Williams and Taylor York, guitarists, are now the only members of the band still releasing albums and hosting concerts. Williams is known for her excentric outfits and energetic stage performances.I was drawn to Paramore's early music because it was powerful and encourages people to be strong. The development of their music has ranged from alternative rock to different variations of pop. They are my favorite band because of the variety and originality of their music.
